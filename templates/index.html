How does it work?

1

Search

Look through current season's list of dramas. Shows are arranged chronologically, so you'll know what's in season and what's coming soon. If you don't find one you like, check the upcoming season's list of dramas!

2

Select

If you see a show you fancy, tap or click the show card to see its detailed information. If it's a match and you like what you're seeing—go ahead and add it to your Kalendar and watchlist!

3

Schedule

On the calendar page, you'll see all your selected shows plotted based on their airing times and dates. Shows are displayed in your local time.

4

Sync

On the watchlist, you can sync your Kalendar to your Google Calendar. If you're done watching, you'll have the option to delete it from your synced calendar too.
