About Kalendar

I just wanna keep track of when my shows get released.

This project started for that reason alone. After Korea started pumping out KDrama after KDrama and my watchlist grew, I got overwhelmed and forgot when the shows I watched weekly would be released. I could list it down manually, but after learning the basics of web development and programming, I realized this was a perfect use-case of what I've studied so far.

I hope that Kalendar will help you in the same way it's helped me. Kalendar is for everyone who needs structure in the chaotic world of KDramas. If you have any questions or feedback, kindly send an email to kdramacalendar2021@gmail.com.

Now to catch up on the episodes I've missed while working on this. Annyeong!
